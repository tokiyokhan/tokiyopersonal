Bachelor of Science in Computer Science( General Computing)

(old name of degree was BCS (Hons.) changed to BS (CS) according to UGC recommendation) Duration: 4 years Degree Program (8 Semesters). Minimum 2nd division in F.Sc. Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical (with one deficiency Math subject) , Intermediate in General Science, Computer Science This four-year degree program provides challenging, in-depth study in the areas that form the basis of computer technology, namely computer programming and computer systems. This program assures that the graduates are adequately prepared to enter and continue to practice in the field of computer science. The program provides a sufficiently broad base of mathematics, physical science, engineering science, computer experience, laboratory experience, and design experience in order that the students can learn to apply the fundamentals of computer science principles in a reasonable and efficient manner. The students are encouraged to design, develop, debug and test software and hardware for computer systems, embedded systems, network systems, and electronic equipments.

Admission Requirements
  1. Pre-requisites:

    2. Minimum 50% marks in F.Sc. Pre-Engineering, Pre-Medical (with one deficiency Math subject), ICS, F.Sc. General Science with Mathematics, FA with Mathematics, or equivalent degrees from recognized institutions.

  2. Procedure for Admission:
    • The list of selected candidates will be displayed on the Notice Board according to the merit formula and schedule notified.
    • Candidates may be admitted in the beginning of the Fall semester. If a selected candidate fails to enroll in time as notified his admission will stand cancelled and seat will be offered to the next candidate on the waiting list.
  3. Merit Scholarships:

    4. Students with A grades (or high First Division) will be considered for 10 Merit Scholarships, which will cover full tuition.
